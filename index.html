What We Do

Camps

Although our camps are more geared towards children between grades 4-8, it is free to all and everyone is welcome to join! At our camps, students learn the basics of the market and gain a solid foundation in not just investing, but many other areas of finance as well.

Stock Market Challenge

Our stock market challenge is free and open to everyone attending our camp! This challenge is a way to put to test what our students have learned from our camp. Prizes will be awarded to the students who have the most money at the end of the challenge!

Impact

Our aim with both of these events is to inspire children of all ages to realize the potential of investing. We want to make this learning process fun and exciting for our children to make sure they retain all the information that they need to accurately invest.

About Us

Our Mission

Many people think of investing and the stock market as risky, dangerous, and even gambling. However, if invested correctly, your wealth can generate more wealth. The best part about that is that you don't have to work for it - your welath will increase without your efforts!

Our aim is to rid this barrier and encourage people of all ages to start investing. Time is the most essential part in investing, and as you lose time, you decrease the your returns from the stock market. We aim to provide this for free as we believe that investing should be something everyone should be able to learn.
